About EcoSort AI

EcoSort AI is an innovative AI-powered waste segregation system designed to help individuals and communities identify and sort waste materials accurately. By leveraging advanced machine learning algorithms, our platform analyzes images of waste to classify them into categories such as Plastic, Metal, Organic, Paper, Glass, and more.

Our mission is to promote environmental sustainability by encouraging proper waste disposal and recycling practices. Whether you're at home, school, or work, EcoSort AI makes it easy to contribute to a greener planet.

Developed for hackathon purposes, this application demonstrates the potential of AI in environmental technology.
